Thaproban Pavillion Resort & Spa 



Treat yourself to the finest luxury comforts of total quietude in 16 spacious Suite rooms with spectacular views across the Galle harbour
 and Dutch fort, with divine cuisine & services designed to indulge your senses in total privacy, set amidst a rocky outcrop in the famous 
Unawatuna beach, Sri Lanka.

The beautifully designed Taproban Pavilion, offering a contemporary blend of the Dutch traditional architecture, where the guests could
 lounge beneath the cooling green coconut groves with a stunning back drop of the Premier meeting the sky, while sipping an aperitif
 in Dutch induced verandah or the stylish bar. The restaurant at the Taproban Pavilion in Unawatuna is an experience in itself, all these
 offer the guests an exclusive environment in which to stay while visiting the beautiful Unwatuna beach in Sri Lanka.

The Dutch fort stands sentinel across the Galle bay, appearing harmonious to the surrounding blue waters, a statement of an era gone
 by, a time of naval warfare, and plundering. Today the sheer loveliness of the breathtaking view & tranquility of the setting is so 
absorbing, read a book by the tropical garden, heal your body and soul with ancient herbal Ayurvedic treatments from our Spa, awake
 to Yoga and meditate in calm surroundings and experience the natural beauty of Unawatuna beach.

This is another elite project by Thambapanni leisure, the owners of exclusivity ...Retreat to Taproban Pavilions for a memorable holiday,
 where luxurious comfort is the way of life......
